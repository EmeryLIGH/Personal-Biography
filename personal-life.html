Personal Life

Peter Griffin is married to Lois Griffin, with whom he shares three children: Meg, Chris, and Stewie. His relationships are often comedic but show his deep love and commitment to his family. Outside of his home life, Peter enjoys drinking at The Drunken Clam with his friends, Quagmire, Cleveland, and Joe.
