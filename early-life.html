Early Life

Peter Griffin was born in Quahog, Rhode Island, to Thelma and Francis Griffin. Growing up in a chaotic household, Peter developed a strong sense of humor and resilience. He was known for getting into trouble from a young age, often with his best friend, Quagmire.
